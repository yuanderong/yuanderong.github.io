Visiting Hangzhou, Nanjing and my hometown Lvsi Harbor

Spring 2018

Hangzhou and Nanjing are two beautiful cities in eastern China. My hometown Lvsi Harbor is a coastal fishing town located on the north shore of the Yangtze River.

Here's a video of my trip:

I was born in Lvsi 55 years ago and left at the age of 15. This journey took me from Toronto to Pudong, and back home to visit my family and friends.

Mom still rides her bicycle around town at 80 years old!
